What We Do

SHC ADVANTAGE SERVICES, INC. takes pride in its partnership with healthcare organizations, collaborating to foster healthier communities by delivering impactful services through our skilled nursing and allied health care professionals.

We offer highly skilled and qualified candidates, backed by more than 15 years of healthcare staffing experience.

Our team of staffing experts ensures meticulous vetting and personalized matching for our clients, providing the highest level of support to meet their staffing requirements.

Together, we are dedicated to fostering healthier communities by delivering exceptional services through our skilled nursing and allied health team.

Temporary Staffing

SHC provides temporary healthcare staff to fill short-term vacancies or address seasonal demand, such as flu seasons or peak patient loads.

Permanent Placement

SHC assists healthcare facilities in the recruitment and placement of permanent staff, helping to fill critical and ongoing positions within the organization.

Locum Tenens Services

SHC facilitates the placement of locum tenens professionals and other healthcare providers to cover temporary absences or staffing shortages.

Travel Nursing Services

SHC offers travel nursing programs, where qualified nurses can take on temporary assignments in different locations, providing flexibility and varied experiences.

Recruitment and Placement

SHC identifies and recruits qualified healthcare professionals by conducting thorough screening, interviews, and credential verification to ensure competence and qualifications of candidates.

Allied Health Staffing

SHC provides allied health professionals such as physical therapists, occupational therapists, radiologic technologists, and other specialized healthcare roles.
