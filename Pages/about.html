Permanent Placement

SHC assists healthcare facilities in the recruitment and placement of permanent staff, helping to fill critical and ongoing positions within the organization.

Who we are

SHC is a leading healthcare staffing agency catering to all specialty areas within the healthcare sector. We specialize in recruiting, placing, and forming strong partnerships with travel nurses, travel healthcare professionals, and other traveling medical professionals.

Our distinction lies in our commitment to tailoring high-paying travel assignments that precisely match the unique skillsets of each travel healthcare professional we work with. Our affiliations extend to the top facilities across New York, managed and operated by industry experts.

At SHC, we are driven to provide the best possible travel experience and take pride in being an integral part of every travel healthcare professional’s journey.

What our company does differently from our competitors?

  • SHC Advantage Services is not focused on driving numbers, but on delivering quality healthcare professionals to facilities we support.
  • We make sure that our caregivers are always up-to-date with their credentials, using our technology platform BlueSky to notify any caregiver of upcoming critical dates.
  • We take seriously, our obligation to interact with facilities and monitor our caregivers’ performances at their deployed facilities.
  • We are very proactive in ensuring that our professional caregivers provide quality care to our patients and communities.
  • We are in the process of offering our Facilities/Clients the opportunity to use our self-service portal to access our pool of ready-to-deploy caregivers directly and fulfill an urgent need 24/7/365.

What we do

As a healthcare staffing agency SHC plays a crucial role in connecting healthcare professionals with healthcare facilities in need of their services.

At SHC ADVANTAGE SERVICES, INC. we take pride in its partnership with healthcare organizations, collaborating to foster healthier communities by delivering impactful services through our skilled nursing and allied healthcare professionals.

We offer highly skilled and qualified candidates, backed by more than 15 years of healthcare staffing experience. Our team of staffing experts ensures meticulous vetting and personalized matching for our clients, providing the highest level of support to meet their staffing requirements.
